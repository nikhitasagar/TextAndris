This application has been created to help the people who sometimes forget their friends and relatives birthdays and anniversaries. They can also wish them on scheduled holidays and festivals. This application will help you schedule and SMS at an exact time and date when you want to send that SMS and will automatically send it from your cellular device at the time you have specified. The user can edit and delete the scheduled messages before they are sent and a log will be provided to view your sent messages. The user can also use a Clear Log function to remove the bulk which is not required from your log.
